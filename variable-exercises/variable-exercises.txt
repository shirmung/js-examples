The Fortune Teller

Why pay a fortune teller when you can just program your fortune yourself?

Store the following in variables: number of children, partner's name, geographic location, and job title.
Output your fortune to the screen like so: "You will be a X in Y, and married to Z with N kids."

The Age Calculator

Want to find out how old you'll be? Calculate it!

Store your birth year in a variable.
Store a future year in a variable.
Calculate your 2 possible ages for that year based on the stored values. For example, if you were born in 1988, then in 2026 you'll be either 37 or 38, depending on what month it is in 2026.
Output them to the screen like so: "I will be either NN or NN in YYYY.", substituting the values.

The Geometrizer

Calculate the properties of a circle.

Store a radius in a variable.
Calculate the circumference based on the radius and output "the circumference is NN".
Calculate the area based on the radius and output "the area is NN".
